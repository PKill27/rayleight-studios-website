Rayleight Studios Announces Don't Crack! — A Vibrant, Rage-Inducing Platformer About Flying Home in a Fragile Eggshell

Fast-Paced Cartoon-Styled Platformer Where Players Take Flight as an Unhatched Chick Navigating the World from Inside Its Fragile Eggshell

Newport Beach, CA – March 6, 2026 – Rayleight Studios, an independent game development studio founded by solo developer Porter Killian, is excited to announce Don't Crack!, a fast-paced cartoon-styled platformer where players take flight as an unhatched chick navigating the world from inside its fragile eggshell. Blending precision controls, chaotic environments, and a playful art style, Don't Crack! delivers a unique mix of challenge, charm, and pure panic.

In Don't Crack!, players flap their tiny breakout wings through a series of vibrant stylistic environments while racing the clock, mastering tight flight mechanics, and fighting to stay un-cracked. Designed to be both joyful and rage-inducing, the game offers a satisfying skill curve that rewards precision, quick thinking, and perseverance.

"Games have always been a source of joy and connection for me. With Don't Crack!, I wanted to build something that feels playful on the surface but demands real mastery underneath — a game that makes you laugh, yell, and immediately hit 'retry.'"

Porter Killian, Developer & Founder
Rayleight Studios

Players can take on the challenge solo or team up in co-op mode for double the chaos. Global leaderboards encourage competition, while the game's expressive cartoon aesthetic keeps the experience lighthearted even when the difficulty spikes.

Don't Crack! represents the debut title from Rayleight Studios, showcasing Porter Killian's ability to create both the art and code behind his games. With a background in Computer Science and Art from the University of Pennsylvania, Killian blends creativity with technical precision to craft playful, challenging experiences.

Key Features

  • Fly as a Fragile Egg: Take control of an unhatched chick navigating the world from inside its delicate shell
  • Precision Platforming: Master tight, responsive flight mechanics that reward skill and timing
  • Cartoon-Styled Chaos: Explore vibrant environments full of personality and playful danger
  • Rage-Inducing Challenges: Push your limits through fast-paced, unforgiving levels
  • Solo or Co-Op Play: Tackle the journey alone or with a friend for twice the chaos
  • Race the Clock: Perfect your routes and beat level timers
  • Climb the Leaderboards: Compete globally for the fastest times and ultimate bragging rights
  • A Journey Home: Guide your fragile hero across diverse environments in a quest to return to the nest

About Rayleight Studios

About Rayleight Studios

Rayleight Studios is an independent game development studio founded by solo developer Porter Killian. Based in Newport Beach, California, the studio focuses on creating vibrant, cartoon-styled games that blend creativity with technical precision. Don't Crack! is the studio's debut title, marking the beginning of its journey into indie game development. Porter Killian creates both the art and code behind his games, with a background in Computer Science and Art from the University of Pennsylvania.
